About Us

SAP is at the center of today’s technology revolution, developing innovations that not only help our customers run at their best, but also improve the lives of people everywhere.

As the market leader in enterprise application software, we help organizations of all sizes and industries combat the damaging effects of complexity, generate new opportunities for innovation and growth, and stay ahead of the competition.

By delivering real time analytics around race rankings, speeds, maneuvers, bearings etc., SAP is helping to simplify and demystify the complex sport of sailing.

SAP Sailing Analytics provide insights and transparency to the world of sailing by utilizing Cloud and In-Memory Technology, processing GPS and wind measurement data in real time and visualizing contents in various frontends accessible from anywhere.
